Why donate blood?

• Blood is the most precious gift that anyone can give to another person, it is the gift of life.

• A decision to donate your blood can save a life, or even several.

• Regular blood donation by a sufficient number of healthy people is needed to ensure that blood will always be available whenever and wherever it is needed.
